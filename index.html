About Me

My name is Ishika Garg and I make websites.

I have a strong understanding of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap and Jquery, enabling me to convert designs into responsive web experiences seamlessly. One of my notable achievements during this training was the successful completion of a project called "Money Tigers," where I developed the entire application individually.In addition to my training, I have actively pursued self-improvement through various online courses, including Internshala, FreeCodeCamp, and SoloLearn.
